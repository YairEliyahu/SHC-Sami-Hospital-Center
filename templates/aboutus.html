SHC-Sami Hospital Center

Information

During the years of its existence, SHC - University Medical Center, became from a small peripheral hospital to a university medical center, one of the leaders in the State of Israel:
A center of excellence for providing available and high-quality medical service to the residents of the Negev on their various levels

  • leader in advanced technologies
  • A leader in research and development
  • Adheres to the cultivation of human capital and the preservation of human dignity and rights.
The SHC Medical Center leads the hospitals in Israel in considering the national quality indicators established by the Ministry of Health.
The SHC hospital is a leader among the largest hospitals in Israel in an inspection conducted by the Ministry of Health in 2017.
The medical center is a leader in a large number of fields of activity, advanced services and research:
The Division of Neurology - an all-inclusive unit that coordinates the diagnostic, treatment and research activities in the various professions dealing with brain disease
  • Heart Center - Cardiothoracic surgery (surgery and cardiology)
  • Bariatric treatment - surgeries to treat morbid obesity
  • A national unit for infectious diseases in children that is the leader in research in Israel and the world
  • Infection Prevention Unit
  • burn unit
  • Intensive care units - general internal and pediatric - with world-class clinical results
  • The largest trauma room in Israel - eight treatment stations including a special station for treating children.
  • The experienced trauma teams in Soroka treat about 600 trauma cases a year
  • The delivery room at SHC is the most productive in the country with more than 17,000 births a year and has a highly experienced medical team.
