MATERNITY DEPARTMENT





There are four maternity wards at Shaare Zedek Medical Center and two at Bikur Cholim.

Each ward has spacious rooms, up to three beds per room.

Each room is equipped with comfortable nursing chairs, personal dressers, a bathroom and a shower.

babies are treated at the nurseries attached to the maternity wards, or at the NICU if needed.

For your comfort, we offer you bathrobes, towels, regular and frozen pads (to alleviate swelling around the stitches), a kitchenette

with a water cooler, hot beverages, a fridge for personal food items and a detailed instruction brochure about caring for

yourself and your baby (please ask the nurse for this).

In addition to regular hospitalization post-partum you can choose the rooming-in method, in which the baby stays with the
